Connect a Display to Wi-Fi

Make sure your Bluetooth is on, then tap the button below and pick your display from the list. The display's screen will say FRC-Display-XXXXXX; pick the one matching your screen.

Tip: not seeing your display?

  • The display must be on its WiFi Setup screen (orange "WiFi Setup" header).
  • Tap Identify in the device list to flash a screen on the projector and confirm you picked the right one.
  • If the picker is empty, fully close Chrome and reopen this page.

Your browser can't do this

Bluetooth setup needs Web Bluetooth, which only works in Google Chrome on Android (or desktop Chrome / Edge). Brave, Firefox, Samsung Internet, and any iOS browser don't support it.

Chrome (Android) Chrome (Desktop) Edge (Desktop)

Use these alternatives instead

  1. Join the display's WiFi network (FRC-Display-XXXXXX) and follow the captive-portal sign-in.
  2. Or put a wifi.ini file at the root of any USB stick: 
    ssid=YourNetwork
password=secret
    and plug it into the display.
